# Pinstriping a car

## Materials

- Lettering enamel  
- Pinstriping brush (00 to 4 are the range)
- Rags  
- Mineral spirits
- Wax and grease remover
- Reducer

## Paint Information

Oil-based paint is removed with mineral spirits

## New Brush

Rinse it off with warm water and light soap. Let it dry before using it.

Then soak it in oil.
